Activity

Directions

Individually or with a partner, choose an innovation that you would like to explore further. As you learn more about it, make sure you can answer the three questions below.

  • What spurred the innovation?
  • What was the innovation?
  • Why is this significant?

Here are some websites that may help you with your research.

  • The Great Idea Finder | 20th Century Innovation Timeline
  • The Startup Guide | The 40 Greatest Innovations of All Time
  • Forbes | Top 30 Innovations Of The Last 30 Years
  • U.S. Patent and Trademark Database
