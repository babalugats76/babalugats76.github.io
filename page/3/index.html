Welcome

Thank you for attending Putting IT Together - A Tech Program for the Real World!

This Problem of Practice session focuses on the challenges involved in implementing real-world technology programs in our schools, including potential solutions and ways forward.

When / Where:

Agenda

Worth Checking Out

Every year, my Game Design students participate in a Game Design showcase. The best student games are played online by people far and wide to determine the overall winner. This is a great example of the kind of real-world, authentic experiences I cover in this session. Experience it for yourself.
